pakalil manalin thaalithil kadhakalezhuthum thennale
puthiya varikalorthu nilkkave iravu peythuvo

vazhiyoram kozhiyuvathee ilakal than kalaam 
kaathooram kelkkave
ithal pokaathoru thennal kaathanagal pole
kanmunnil kaanave
ere kadhakal aathma kadhakal jeevan thedave
novaay nin sirakalil
